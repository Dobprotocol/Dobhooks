How it works

01

Operators tokenize future revenue

Infrastructure operators (EV chargers, data centers, solar farms) tokenize their future cashflow into yield-bearing ERC-20 tokens on Token Studio.

02

Dob validates performance in real time

AI validator agents monitor machine performance, verify revenue, and produce a live risk score. This data feeds the on-chain oracle.

03

Investors buy verified yield on the DEX

Investors purchase RWA tokens with verified, real-time performance data. Every asset has a transparent risk profile backed by live validation.

04

Programmatic buybacks enable on-demand liquidity

When investors sell, the Uniswap V4 hook executes at oracle-pegged price. Liquidity Nodes provide exit capital for distressed assets.
