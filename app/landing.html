Core Protocol

DeFi Meets Real-World Assets

Purpose-built smart contracts for tokenizing, pricing, swapping, and liquidating RWAs on-chain.

Uniswap V4 Peg Hook

Custom Accounting Hook intercepts swaps via beforeSwap, returns BeforeSwapDelta. PoolManager skips AMM math entirely. Settlement at exact oracle price.

DobPegHook.sol

RWA Vault + dUSDC

Deposit tokenized real-world assets, receive dUSDC minted 1:1 at oracle price. Redeem dUSDC for USDC anytime. The protocol's native stablecoin.

DobRwaVault.sol

Liquidity Nodes

Permissionless LP system. Deposit USDC to provide exit liquidity (earn swap fees) or back specific assets for liquidations (earn discounted RWA tokens). FIFO matching.

DobLPRegistry.sol

Oracle Registry

AI validator-managed oracle prices for every RWA asset. Powers the peg, liquidation thresholds, staleness circuit breakers, and per-asset caps.

DobValidatorRegistry.sol

Liquidation Engine

When AI validators detect distress, LPs fill orders at penalty discount. 33% reserve holds prevent cascading exits. Time-locked withdrawals prevent front-running.

Penalty + FIFO

Safety Rails

Anti-flash-loan protection (MIN_BACKING_AGE), 24h withdrawal timelocks, per-asset and global liquidation caps, oracle staleness circuit breaker.

Process

How It Works

From real-world asset to on-chain stablecoin in four steps.

1

Deposit RWA

Deposit tokenized assets (DCT, SFT, RET, etc.) into the DobRwaVault at oracle price.

2

Mint dUSDC

Receive dUSDC stablecoin minted 1:1 at the oracle-verified price of your asset.

3

Swap at Peg

Swap through Uniswap V4 Hook at 1:1 oracle price. Zero slippage, low fee on sell direction.

4

Redeem or LP

Redeem dUSDC for USDC, provide USDC liquidity to earn swap fees, or back assets to earn discounted RWA tokens from liquidations.
