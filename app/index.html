How Liquidations Work
1. Distress Signal
Dobprotocol AI agents detect asset distress and activate liquidation mode with a penalty rate.
2. FIFO Matching
LP backings are matched in FIFO order. Only LPs whose minPenaltyBps ≤ penalty get filled.
3. Fill & Earn
Your USDC buys distressed RWA at discount. You receive dUSDC at full oracle price — the spread is your profit.
4. Reserve Hold
33% of filled amount is held as reserve for 24h to ensure system stability. Released automatically.

How the Pooled LN Works
1. Deposit
Anyone deposits USDC and receives shares proportional to their contribution.
2. Operator Backs
Dobprotocol decides which RWA assets to back and sets dynamic discount rates.
3. Fills Happen
When sellers exit, the pool fills at a discount — earning RWA tokens at below-market price.
4. Earn RWA
RWA tokens are distributed to depositors proportionally. These tokens generate APR from the real asset.

Glossary

Oracle Price
The USD price of an RWA token, set by AI validator agents via DobValidatorRegistry. Used for vault deposits/withdrawals. NOT the swap rate.
LP Discount
The spread charged by Liquidity Nodes when you sell. LPs set their own discount rate (minPenaltyBps). This is how LPs profit from providing exit liquidity.
dUSDC
The protocol's intermediate stablecoin. Minted 1:1 at oracle price when you deposit RWA tokens into the vault. Burned when withdrawing.
Liquidity Node
An LP that deposits USDC and backs specific RWA assets. When sellers exit, LPs provide the USDC and receive discounted RWA tokens in return.
Pooled LN
A shared Liquidity Node managed by Dobprotocol. Anyone can deposit USDC and earn a proportional share of RWA tokens from fills. Easier than running your own LP.
Liquidation
When an asset is distressed, Dobprotocol activates liquidation mode with a penalty rate. LPs who back that asset participate in fills at the mandated discount.
Slippage
The difference between expected and actual output. On sells, slippage comes from LP discount variability. Set a tolerance to protect against unexpected price changes.
APR / Yield
RWA tokens generate periodic yield from the underlying real-world asset (rent, energy revenue, etc). Distributed via Token Studio's DistributionPool contracts.
